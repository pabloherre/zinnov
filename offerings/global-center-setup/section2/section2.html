Large companies have found immense value in creating Global Centers of Excellence (GCoE), which extend beyond the traditional GCC/GIC models, to establish expertise, drive innovation, access global talent and markets. While larger organizations leverage value from setting up Global Centers of Excellence, the process involves high upfront investment and usually takes between 6 to 8 months to setup. We are witnessing an increasing number of small and mid-size companies looking for quicker and less cost-intensive solutions to ramp-up their operations. The evolving business scenario has propelled companies to pivot to models that ensure resilience, continuity, and efficiency.

Zinnov’s GCoE Accelerator Model is a “plug- and- play” solution which can be turned-around in as little as 90 days and helps companies rapidly scale new capabilities and ways of working. GCoE Accelerators provide clients with the same benefits are a traditional center but with lower risk, no long-term commitment, lower upfront investment, and ultimately, significantly more flexibility in terms of scale and exit options.
