Anand Kalra is Principal- Investment Banking and is based out of Zinnov’s India headquarters. He is an expert in the firm’s Market Expansion practice (link to offering page) and helms the execution of Global Sell-side/Buy-side M&A, Capital Market Solutions, Joint Ventures & Partnerships, Due Diligence, Corporate Finance and Corporate Strategy.
At Zinnov, Anand works extensively across a wide range of engagements valued at over $40bn, with a global network of Fortune 500 technology companies that comprises of large digital platform companies, digital service providers and end-user organizations. His key focus areas also include small and large Automation and Software companies.
Passionate about helping clients deliver breakthrough results, Anand works closely with our clients to build unyielding value in financial services that drive business transformation, with cross-industry expertise spanning a range of sectors including Telecommunications, BFSI, Automotive, Retail, IoT, Automation, Healthcare and Software.
With more than 13 years of experience serving clients spanning across geographies including North America, Europe, India, China, and Singapore, Anand is an empathetic leader, customer champion and expert negotiator, with strong analytical and project management skills which help him deliver guaranteed high-value results.
Prior to Zinnov, Anand was Director at HSBC (New York) wherein he played a pivotal role in pioneering the IoT/ Industrial Software IB coverage and spearheaded the efforts globally. Additionally, he has worked at Deloitte (India) as a Strategy Research consultant. Over the course of his career, Anand has served as an advocate for building cross-global, inclusive and diverse teams, and is a sought-after speaker and mentor within the ecosystem.
Anand received an MBA from FORE School of Management and is a Gold Medalist in B.Sc – Maths Hons from MDS University, India.
